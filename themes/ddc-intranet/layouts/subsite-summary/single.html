Capability Whitepapers

Start Writing

Whitepapers are a longer-form medium suitable for in-depth exploration of DDC capabilities, innovations and thought leadership on a variety of topics. Whitepapers exist as a specific type of artifact within DDC's canon of shared, tangible intellectual capital and are carefully positioned between the short-form, marketing like language found in a capability slick and the more comprehensive treatment of a given discipline found in a capability delivery playbook. To that end, a whitepaper will typically have a corresponding capability slick and be directly related to a larger discipline covered by one of our delivery capability playbooks. This provides our SMEs, technologists and functional experts across DDC a great deal of flexibility in the topics they can address while still staying well within DDC strategic guardrails.

Capability Playbooks

Start Writing

Capability Delivery Playbooks are a type of DDC shared intellectual capital which represent an encapsulation of DDC's methodology or approach to execution and carry the DDC Spectrum branding. Playbooks are developed to address key practices areas within each of DDC's Digital Transformation themed focus areas including Data Enablement, Data Science, Cloud Adoption, Modern Software Development and ITSM/DevOps. Inasmuch as Playbook content is targeted towards guiding and ensuring quality delivery, most Playbook content will be considered company proprietary. Our Innovation Capability Leads are responsible for identifying, prioritizing and leading Playbook development based on their assigned area of focus.

Capability Slicks

Start Writing

Capability Slicks are one page, selling focused documents which summarizes the differentiated value proposition of a specific capability, technique, process, methodology or innovation provided by our DDC family of companies. These documents are designed to be printed on high quality paper or card stock and serve as introductory or leave-behind materials for existing or potential clients. These documents have also proven to be a reliable source of ready-made, cut-n-paste materials for RFI's, SOOs and other brief response opportunities. Delivery teams can use these documents to support tactical selling opportunities.
